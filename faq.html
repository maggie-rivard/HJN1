FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

When is Alcohol Served and Sold?

Alcohol is served and sold daily from 9:00am until midnight.

Alcohol is not allowed ot be served or sold between midnight and 9:00am.

What size Power Outlets are on the island?

Power outlets are 120 volts.

These are the same power outlets as in the United States.

What is the drinking age?

The legal drinking age in Taniti is 18 years old.

The drinking age is not strictly enforced.

Do Tanitians speak English?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English.

Very little English is spoken in rural area, especially by the older residents.

Are there hospitals on the island?

There is one hospital and several clinics on the island.

The hospital has many multilingual enployees.

Is there crime on the island?

Violent crime is very rare on the island.

As tourism increases there are more reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.
